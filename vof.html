Valley Of Flowers


Duration - 7 days / 6 nights
Difficulty - Easy to Moderate
Age Group - 15-35 years
Max Altitude - 15,197 ft


Places:

Day:1

Journey towards Govindghat

Day:2

Trek to Ghangharia

Day:3

This trek has a difficulty level from moderate to difficult. Valley of Flowers is known to be the heaven of the Earth. The Valley of Flowers has recorded 600 species of angiosperms and 30 pteridophytes in the valley and surroundings.

Day:4

Trek to Hemkund Sahib

Day:5

The same day, the camp departs for Badrinath. Badrinath is one of the four ‘Char Dham’. Badrinath is the temple of Lord Vishnu

Day:6

Return Journey
